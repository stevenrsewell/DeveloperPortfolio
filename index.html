About Me

My name is Steven Robert Sewell. I was born and raised in Keyport New Jersey, part of the greater New York City Metro area. I attended elementary and middle school at St. Joseph School in Aberdeen Township, and high school at Saint John Vianney in Holmdel.

In 2010 I graduated high school, and moved to Tampa Florida to attend the University of South Florida. I took classes at both USF's Tampa and Saint Petersburg campuses, and majored in Health Sciences. While living in Tampa, my favorite things to do in the city were going to the beach, going to Busch Gardens, and watch Tampa Bay Lightning hockey games at Amalie Arena.

After completing my bachelor's degree, I moved to Austin Texas and began working in the health and fitness industry. I earned a personal trainer certification from the National Academy of Sports Medicine, and a CrossFit Level-1 Trainer certification. For a few years I worked as a personal trainer and nutritional consultant, and in my free time enjoyed hiking and kayaking in the Austin area. In 2020 I completed a Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at the University of Texas Center for Professional Education.

In December 2021, I moved back to my hometown of Keyport, New Jersey. Since returning I have started working on several personal projects, including forming a family consulting business, launching a YouTube gaming channel, and creating a recreational photography brand called Crowned One Photography.

Some of my hobbies and activities including landscape and aerial photography, going to live NFL, MLB, NHL, WWE, and AEW events in the New York City area, aviation, and going hiking and visiting waterfront parks with my shiba inu, Kuma.
