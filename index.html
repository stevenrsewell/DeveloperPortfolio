About Me

My name is Steven Robert Sewell. I was born and grew up in Keyport New Jersey, part of the New York City Metropolitan area. I went to elementary and middle school in Aberdeen Township, and attended Saint John Vianney High School in Holmdel.

In 2010 I graduated high school and moved to Tampa Florida to attend the University of South Florida. I took classes at both USF's Tampa and Saint Petersburg campuses, and majored in Health Sciences. During this time I also worked as a youth sports coach and referee for i9 Sports. While in Tampa, my favorite things to do were going to Busch Gardens, going to the beach, and watching Tampa Bay Lightning hockey games at Amalie Arena.

After completing my bachelor's degree I moved to Austin Texas and started working in the health and fitness industry. I earned a personal trainer certification from the National Academy of Sports Medicine, and a CrossFit Level-1 Trainer certification. I worked in settings including a fitness facility, a physical therapy clinic, and a wellness resort, focusing on nutritional consulting and exercise training. I spent my free time hiking and kayaking in the Austin area, and enjoying the live music scene and diverse foods in the city.

In 2020 I completed a Full Stack Developer coding bootcamp at the University of Texas. Following this I began working at LegalZoom, where I gained experience in business formation, compliance, and entrepreneurship. At the end of 2021 I moved back to my hometown of Keyport, NJ. Since then I have started several personal projects including forming a family consulting business, creating a recreational photography brand, launching a sports blog, and forming an eSports gaming YouTube channel.

Some of my hobbies and activities including landscape and aerial photography, aviation, going to live NFL, MLB, NHL, WWE, and AEW events in the New York City area, and visiting waterfront parks with my dog Kuma.
